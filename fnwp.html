Five Nights With Prostitytes

Genre: Survival horror, strategy

Platform: PC Windows, Android

Multiplayer: Single player

Status: Available

Five Nights With Prostitytes (FNWP) is a classic survival horror. You are a guard hired at the abandoned entertainment complex 'Prostitytes World'. For five nights, you must survive from midnight to 6 AM, fending off out-of-control sex animatronics. Use the camera system, control office doors, and monitor your power supply.

🎮 Core Mechanics

  • Camera system — track animatronics' movement throughout the complex
  • Door control — close office doors to block passage
  • Limited power — every action consumes energy, use wisely
  • Interference system — animatronics can cause static interference on cameras
  • Individual attack scenarios — each animatronic has a unique scene when caught
  • Dynamic difficulty — AI adapts to player's tactics
  • Audio lore — find hidden audio recordings revealing the complex's history

🤖 Sex Animatronics

Fexa

Fox animatronic. Cunning and calculating.

🎭 Behavior: Moves stealthily, often appears in vents. Can block cameras in her sector.
⚠️ Weakness: Noise grenades temporarily disorient her.

Fnia

Bear of a different model line. Aggressive and direct.

🎭 Behavior: Attacks through the main corridor. Her steps are heard from afar. High speed.
⚠️ Weakness: Requires constant visual monitoring through cameras.

China

Chicken animatronic. Unpredictable behavior.

🎭 Behavior: Can appear in the most unexpected places. Mimics equipment malfunction sounds.
⚠️ Weakness: Chaotic movement pattern — hard to track, but can be fooled.

Bonfi

Rabbit animatronic. Uses psychological pressure.

🎭 Behavior: Freezes motionless on cameras. Causes interference on monitors.
⚠️ Weakness: Active camera switching forces her to retreat.
