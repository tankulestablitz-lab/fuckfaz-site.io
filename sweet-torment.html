Sweet Torment

Genre: Co-op horror, stealth, dating sim, shooter

Platform: PC Windows, Android

Multiplayer: LAN co-op (2-4 игрока)

Status: In active development

Sweet Torment is a co-op horror with an erotic twist. You and your friends find yourselves in different locations where you need to survive, collect items, and avoid being caught by the monsters. If caught — a scene triggers. LAN multiplayer supported.

🎮 Core Mechanics

  • Stealth — hide in shadows, don't make noise, avoid eye contact
  • Collect keys/items — find 3 keys to open the exit
  • Each monster has unique mechanics: reacts to light, sound, smell, etc.
  • When caught, a scene with animation and voiceover triggers
  • Random level selection on each launch
  • LAN co-op — play with friends, save each other
